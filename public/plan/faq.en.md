# Frequently Asked Questions

## What age is this for?

From elementary school through high school. As long as you can read and write, you can enjoy using AI tools. Younger children benefit from joining with a parent or guardian.

## Do parents need to be involved?

Not required, but recommended! Especially for younger children, working together deepens understanding and makes the experience more fun.

## What payment methods are accepted?

We accept credit cards, PayPay, and other payment methods. Details are shown during the sign-up process.

## How do I cancel?

Message us on Slack or email us (garoop.company@gmail.com) and we'll stop billing from the following month. No cancellation fees, ever.

## Is there a free trial?

We don't currently offer a free trial. However, the Super plan at ¥330/month is very affordable — it's a great way to try Garoop at minimal cost.

## How hard are the challenges?

Challenges start at a beginner-friendly level. Every challenge comes with hint videos so you'll never be stuck. Pro plan members can also ask questions directly.

## Can I use it on a smartphone?

Yes! Most content works on smartphones. Some AI tools are better on a PC, but the majority of the experience is mobile-friendly.

## Where are creations published?

Works are posted on GaroopTV (garooptv.com) where family and friends can view them.

## What languages are supported for questions?

We support Japanese, English, and Indonesian. We'll do our best to help in other languages too.

## How much does the VIP plan cost?

The VIP plan is individually priced after a consultation. Please reach out to discuss your needs.
