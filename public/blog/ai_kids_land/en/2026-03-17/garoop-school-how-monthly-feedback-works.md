## Self-Paced First, Review Once A Month

Garoop School is not built around live classes every time. Learners mainly move forward on their own through videos and assignments, then use one monthly feedback session to adjust direction.

## What We Review In Feedback

* What the learner created
* Where progress got stuck
* What should be improved next

## Combined With Garoop AI And LINE

Daily questions can be handled through Garoop AI and LINE. The monthly meeting then pulls everything together so learning does not stop between assignments.
