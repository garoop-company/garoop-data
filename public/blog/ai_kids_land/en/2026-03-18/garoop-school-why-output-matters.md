## Knowledge Alone Does Not Become a Weapon

At Garoop School, knowing how to use generative AI is not enough. Real ability grows only through a cycle of making something, publishing it, receiving reactions, and improving it again.

## Build Strength Through Small Weekly Practice

* Turn ideas into one concrete piece each week
* Prioritize publishing over perfection
* Use reactions as the next source of improvement

## A Foundation For Independent Learners

When children get used to creating output every week, AI stops being a passive study tool. It becomes a practical tool for expressing their own thinking and acting on it.
