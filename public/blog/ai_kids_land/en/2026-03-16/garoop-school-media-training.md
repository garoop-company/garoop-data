## Making Is Not The Finish Line

Even if learners can create something with generative AI, nothing comes back unless they deliver it to the outside world. At Garoop School, publishing is treated as part of training, not as an optional extra.

## What Media Training Builds

* Thinking about who the work is for
* Improving titles and presentation
* Learning from audience reactions

## Why GaroopTV Matters

Adding GaroopTV beside X, YouTube, and Note creates one more place to show student work and challenges. More channels make the learning process more practical.
